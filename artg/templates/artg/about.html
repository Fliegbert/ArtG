About

I like painting myself, so i wanted to create a webpage for myself, where i could show and comment on my favorite artists and inspirations. Since i'am interested in artists and art history kinda, i thought it would be a good idea to make a webpage, where i could go on in case i'm border or just in the mood to learn and rediscover stuff in the art world.

There are many Resources out there i think are worth using. So i want to share some and explain why i like them and also i want to have something like a collection.
